# Programmatic SEO Framework & Landing Page Strategy

## Objective
To capture high-intent organic search traffic by programmatically generating long-tail landing pages targeting specific emotional states, situations, and mental wellness queries.

## Keyword Strategy (Long-Tail Focus)
Instead of competing for broad terms like "mental health app," we target hyper-specific, situation-based queries.

**Pattern 1: "How to [cope with / handle / journal about] [Specific Emotion / Situation]"**
- *Examples:* "How to journal about work burnout", "How to cope with midnight anxiety", "How to process grief through reflection"

**Pattern 2: "AI [Companion / Chat / Journal] for [Audience / Problem]"**
- *Examples:* "Private AI journal for overthinkers", "Judgment-free AI companion for social anxiety"

## Landing Page Architecture
Each programmatic landing page will follow a standardized, high-conversion template:

1. **Hero Section:** Empathy-driven headline matching the search intent (e.g., "Can't sleep because of racing thoughts? Let's talk."). Call-to-action (CTA): "Download the App for Free".
2. **Value Proposition (The 'Why'):** Explain *why* traditional journaling or social media isn't helping, and how a private, offline AI companion solves the problem.
3. **Interactive Demo/Screenshot:** A high-quality mockup of the app's relevant feature (e.g., Nocturnal Mode for sleep queries).
4. **Authority Section:** A brief excerpt from our Educational Articles linking to the science or psychology behind the coping mechanism.
5. **Trust Badges:** "100% Offline Storage", "No Ads", "No Judgment".
6. **Footer CTA:** Final prompt to start the free Sanctuary tier.
