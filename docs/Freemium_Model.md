# Holistic Life Companion: Freemium Model & Pricing Strategy

## Philosophy
The app operates on a "Trust-First" and "Value-First" model. Core emotional support features are available for free to ensure accessibility for those in need, while premium features offer deeper analytics, customization, and advanced AI capabilities.

## Free Tier (The "Sanctuary")
Designed to provide immediate value and emotional support without friction.
- **Daily Reflection:** Unlimited text entries and basic mood tracking.
- **Memory Jar:** Store up to 50 memories with basic text search.
- **Supportive Chat:** Limited to 10 AI responses per day. Standard empathetic model.
- **Nocturnal Companion:** 1 default ambient soundscape (White Noise) and basic dream journal logging.

## Premium Tier (The "Oasis") - $7.99/month or $59.99/year
Targeted at users seeking deeper personal insights, customized experiences, and proactive mental wellness tools.
- **Unlimited Memory Jar:** Unlimited storage, rich media uploads (images/audio), and AI-generated "Memory Lane" summaries.
- **Unlimited Supportive Chat:** Unrestricted chat with priority AI processing.
- **Voice Profiles:** Access to all voice personas (Aura, Echo, Sage) with active listening and spoken responses.
- **Advanced Nocturnal Mode:** Full library of binaural beats, deep rain, and sleep frequencies. AI dream analysis and pattern recognition.
- **Emotional Analytics:** Monthly mood reports, trigger identification, and customized coping strategy generation.
- **Email Assistance:** AI drafting for difficult conversations, apologies, or professional boundaries based on chat context.

## Conversion Strategy
- **Soft Paywalls:** Allow users to test Premium features (e.g., one free voice session or one dream analysis) before prompting an upgrade.
- **Milestone Rewards:** Offer a 7-day Premium trial after a user completes a 7-day daily reflection streak.
